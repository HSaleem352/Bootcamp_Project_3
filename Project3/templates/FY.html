Age distribution by Covid severity level

Our analysis revealed a noteworthy correlation between age and COVID-19 severity in breast cancer patients. Individuals aged 49 to 68 tended to experience milder symptoms, while those in the age range of 58 to 79 exhibited more moderate symptoms.
Severe cases were more prevalent in individuals aged 57 to 79. This insight emphasizes the importance of considering age as a factor in predicting COVID-19 outcomes among breast cancer patients.
